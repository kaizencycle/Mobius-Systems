# Bylaws (v2)

## 1. Council & TSC

- **Concord Council:** 7 seats, 1-year terms, staggered; oversees ethics, licensing, anti-capture.
- **TSC:** 5–11 maintainers; owns technical roadmap, releases, reference implementations.
- Quorum: Council (≥5), TSC (≥50% + 1). Conflict resolution: joint session; if tie → defer to Charter invariants.

## 2. Elections & Removal

- Anonymous STV vote; eligibility: ≥3 merged PRs or recognized adopter.
- Removal: 2/3 Council for ethics breach; 2/3 TSC for technical negligence.

## 3. Decision Classes

- **Kernel changes:** TSC supermajority (⅔) + Council veto window (7 days).
- **License or policy changes:** Council supermajority (⅔) + public RFC.
- **Releases:** TSC simple majority + signed attestation.

## 4. Transparency

- Minutes, RFCs, and release artifacts posted in `docs/governance` and releases page.
