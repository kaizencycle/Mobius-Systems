# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We commit to a respectful, inclusive community built on the principles of integrity, transparency, and collaboration that underpin Mobius Systems and the Kaizen Turing Test (KTT).

## Our Standards

### Positive behaviors:
- Respectful and constructive dialogue
- Welcoming diverse perspectives and backgrounds
- Focusing on what's best for the community and the integrity of the platform
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Showing empathy toward other community members

### Unacceptable behaviors:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Trolling, insulting comments, or sustained disruption
- Bad-faith arguments or deliberate misrepresentation
- Attempts to capture or subvert governance processes

## Enforcement

Report issues to **security@mobius.systems** or the designated Foundation contact.

**Enforcement Process:**
1. **Warning** - First violation, corrective action requested
2. **Temporary Ban** - Repeated violations, cooling-off period
3. **Permanent Ban** - Serious or persistent violations (Council vote required)

All enforcement actions are logged and reported in quarterly transparency reports.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Mobius Systems spaces: repositories, forums, events, and official communications.
