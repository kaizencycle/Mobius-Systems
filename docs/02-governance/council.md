# Concord Council

The **Concord Council** is the ethical and policy governance body of the Mobius Systems Foundation.

## Mandate

Guard the Foundation's **integrity principles**:

1. **Charter Compliance** - Ensure all decisions align with Foundation Charter
2. **Ethical Oversight** - Enforce Ethical License Addendum
3. **Anti-Capture** - Monitor and prevent concentration of power
4. **Community Stewardship** - Represent diverse stakeholder interests

## Composition

**7 Seats** (odd number prevents ties):

| Seat | Selection | Term | Eligibility |
|------|-----------|------|-------------|
| 1-5 | Community election | 2 years | Active contributors |
| 6 | Legal/ethics expert | 2 years | Appointed by seats 1-5 |
| 7 | At-large (global diversity) | 2 years | Community election |

**Staggered Terms**: 3 seats elected in odd years, 4 in even years.

## Responsibilities

### Policy & Ethics
- Review RFCs for Charter/ethical compliance
- Approve changes to Code of Conduct
- Enforce Ethical License Addendum
- Investigate reported violations

### Anti-Capture Monitoring
- Review quarterly concentration metrics
- Trigger hard fork if thresholds exceeded
- Approve whale limit exceptions (none granted to date)
- Monitor vendor influence

### Community Oversight
- Publish transparency reports (quarterly)
- Hold open office hours (monthly)
- Respond to community concerns
- Facilitate conflict resolution

### Strategic Guidance
- Long-term vision and roadmap input
- Partnership/affiliation approval
- Budget oversight (with TSC)
- Foundation officer selection (with TSC)

## Decision Process

### Voting
- **Quorum**: 5 of 7 members
- **Standard**: Simple majority (4 votes)
- **Charter changes**: ⅔ majority (5 votes)
- **Hard fork trigger**: ⅔ majority (5 votes)

### Veto Power
Council may **veto** any decision that:
- Violates the Charter
- Undermines anti-capture protections
- Breaches Ethical Addendum
- Concentrates power improperly

Veto requires **⅔ vote** and **written rationale** published within 48 hours.

## Meetings

### Regular
- **Frequency**: Monthly (first Tuesday, 17:00 UTC)
- **Format**: Video call + IRC backchannel
- **Agenda**: Published 7 days prior
- **Notes**: Published within 7 days after

### Emergency
- **Trigger**: Security issue, ethical violation, capture threat
- **Notice**: 48 hours minimum
- **Quorum**: 5 of 7
- **Rationale**: Published when safe to disclose

## Transparency

### Public by Default
- Meeting agendas and notes
- Vote records with rationale
- Policy enforcement actions (anonymized if needed)
- Quarterly transparency reports

### Private Exceptions
- Security vulnerabilities (pre-disclosure)
- Legal proceedings
- Harassment reports
- Competitive-sensitive discussions (rare)

All private sessions **logged and disclosed** when no longer sensitive.

## Term Limits

- **Maximum**: 2 consecutive 2-year terms (4 years)
- **Cooling-off**: 1 term (2 years) before re-eligibility
- **No lifetime limits**: Can return after cooling-off

## Conflict of Interest

Council members must **disclose**:
- Employment by organizations using Mobius
- Consulting/advisory roles related to AI governance
- Financial interests in competing projects
- Personal relationships with TSC members

**Recusal required** for votes where conflict exists.

## Removal

A Council member may be removed by:
- **⅔ vote** of remaining Council members, **OR**
- **Unanimous vote** of TSC
- **Automatic** after missing 3 consecutive meetings

Reasons:
- Code of Conduct violations
- Conflict of interest violations
- Prolonged inactivity
- Capture attempts
